Giving with Confidence: FAQs for Donors

[1] Why is donating important?
+
Donations help support essential programs that alleviate poverty, provide healthcare, and empower communities, creating lasting change.
[2] How are donations used?
+
Funds are allocated to specific projects such as education, health services, and food security, ensuring transparency and impact.
[3] Can I specify where my donation goes?
+
Yes, you can choose to direct your donation to a specific program or initiative that aligns with your interests.
[4] Is my donation tax-deductible?
+
Most donations are tax-deductible. We provide receipts for your contributions for tax purposes.
